My Expertise.


Website Pentesting

I am an experienced and skilled cybersecurity professional with a proven track record of successfully identifying and remediating vulnerabilities in a wide range of websites and web applications. I have worked with various clients, including government agencies, financial institutions, and private companies, to assess and improve the security of their web-based systems.
Over the years, I have identified and helped remediate numerous vulnerabilities in government websites, such as SQL injection, cross-site scripting, and session management issues. In addition to government websites, I have also secured a variety of other websites, including e-commerce sites, social media platforms, and healthcare portals.
My approach to website pentesting involves a thorough analysis of the website or web application, using a combination of manual and automated testing techniques. I am skilled at using a wide range of tools and techniques to identify vulnerabilities, including both common and complex issues.
